2. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Service, we collect the following types of information:

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account Data: Your name, email address, phone number, profile picture, and any other details you provide when creating or updating your account.
  • Verification Data: Information required to verify your identity or, for drivers, your vehicle. This may include images of your National ID, driver's license, or vehicle registration documents. This data is handled with the highest level of security.
  • User Content: The details of Requests you post, Offers you make, ratings and reviews you leave, and messages you send through our in-app chat.

Information We Collect Automatically During Use:

  • Location Data: We collect your device's location while you are actively using the app to enable core features like showing nearby requests, calculating routes for rides, and providing ETAs. You can manage location permissions in your device settings.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Service, such as the features you use, searches you perform, and actions you take.
  • Device and Technical Data: Your IP address, device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and security purposes.

Information from Third Parties:

  • Payment Information: When you make a payment, the transaction is processed by our third-party payment partners. We do not store your full credit card details but may receive limited metadata, such as the last four digits of your card and transaction confirmation.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share it in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: To facilitate a transaction, we share necessary information. For example: A Requester will see a Responder's public profile, rating, and Offer details. For a ride, the Rider and Driver will see each other's name, profile picture, rating, and live location during the trip.
  • With Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party partners for services like cloud hosting, data analytics, payment processing, and SMS/email delivery. These partners are contractually obligated to protect your data and can only use it for the specific services they provide to us.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information to law enforcement, government authorities, or other third parties if we are legally required to do so, or if we believe in good faith that it is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.
  • In a Business Transfer: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to data protection safeguards.

We are committed to protecting your data. We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (SSL/TLS), restricted data access controls, and regular security monitoring. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights Under the Sri Lanka Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)

As a user in Sri Lanka, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • The Right to Rectification: You can request to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The Right to Erasure: You can request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • The Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we process your data based on your consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time.
  • The Right to Object to Processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your data based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@request.lk. We will respond to your request in accordance with the PDPA.

8. Children's Privacy
